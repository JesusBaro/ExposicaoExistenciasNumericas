Idealização
e curatoria

book

The exhibition Numerical Existence brings to Oi Futuro the visualization of data in order to discuss issues present in today's society and technological advances, such as subjectivity, vigilance and the very notion of truth. Data visualization as an emerging artistic language, by distancing itself from a pragmatic practice, allows one to poetically address the complexity and ambiguities of society. This book expands the purpose of the exhibition and brings together texts from leading researchers in the field of data visualization and art.
