WORKSHOP - SMALL DATA

with Barbara Castro and Luiz Ludwig

Infos

inspired int the books 'Dear Data' and 'Observe, Collect and Draw' by Giorgia Lupi and Stefanie Posavec
November 9th at LabSonica from 5:30 p.m. to 8:00 p.m.
Rua Dois de Dezembro, 107. Flamengo
Age :: from 15 years
‍

Proposal

Workshop Small Data will discuss and experience more humanistic and artistic data approaches inspired by the method proposed by Giorgia Lupi and Stefanie Posavec. Lupi is one of the invited researchers that will publish a text excerpt on the book Numerical Existence. The workshop will present an introduction to the methods of representation from the exercise of data collection carried out during a visit to the exhibition with the creators Barbara Castro and Luiz Ludwig, both professors of the Department of Arts & Design of PUC-Rio.
‍

Goals

Introduction to Data Representation Methods'

Exercise of daily data collection

Creation of posters / personal data cards
